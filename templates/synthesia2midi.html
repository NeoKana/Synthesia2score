Our Contribution

This site allows you to convert Synthesia-style videos into MIDI files, which can then be easily turned into sheet music using software like Synthesia. It's the first of its kind to create sheet music directly from Synthesia videos!!

Our tool distinguishes different instruments (instruments are randomly assigned) or right/left hands by color, making it user-friendly.

Note: While the conversion is not perfect and may include minor octave shifts or inaccuracies, it simplifies the process significantly compared to creating sheet music manually from scratch.
